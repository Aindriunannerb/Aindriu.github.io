Audio Visual 1

This is a small portfolio of the projects I worked on during the Audio Visual 1 module in semester three.

Greenway Advertisement for Smarter Travel Competition

This was a short clip made radio ad made for the smarter travel competition. I was given a range of topics to choose from and decided on the Greenway. I wrote the script and enlisted the help of my class mate and lecturer Gianlucca and Colm, for the voice acting. The audio was recorded in the Waterford Institute of Technologys recording studio. The audio samples heard in this ad were found on freesamples.org

Hiphop Mashup for Waterford Vikings

This one minute mashup was created for timeouts during Waterford Vikings basketball games. With time being the limiting factor in this assignment I had to squeeze the essence of each song into about 15 seconds.
